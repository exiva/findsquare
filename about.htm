What?

findsquare is built as a simple tool for foursquare superusers to find venues that are miscategorized, and show up in the Explore function of foursquare taking away from the explore experience.

Why?

findsquare was built because people tagging their homes as Bar's and other nightlife probably in reality don't want you coming over for a beer when you find their home in Explore. (...Probably.)

Who?

Built by Travis La Marr (@exiva) during the 2011 fall foursquare Hackaton. Background image from teamstickergiant under a Creative Commons license

How?

Built using lighttpd, PHP 5.1, jQuery, Sublime Text 2, Pizza, and Arizona Arnold Palmer Iced tea. Hacked together on Long Island
